3. APP PERMISSIONS AND THEIR USE

Our application may request the following permissions only to provide core features:
          
          Internet (INTERNET)
          Used to connect the app with our servers, enable account services, voice calls, and notifications.
          
          Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)
          Used to detect connectivity status to improve call stability and performance.
          
          Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
          Used to send call alerts, messages, and important app updates.
          
          Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)
          Required for voice call functionality. We do not recond / store audio.
          
          Modify Audio Settings (MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS)
          Used to optimise call audio quality and manage speaker or headset behaviour.
          
          Bluetooth / Bluetooth Connect
          Used when connecting audio devices such as wireless earphones during calls.
          
          Foreground Service & Foreground Service Microphone
          Allows ongoing calls to continue while the app is in the background.
          
          Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK)
          Keeps the device awake during active calls to prevent interruption.
          
          Vibration (VIBRATE)
          Used for incoming call alerts and notifications.
          
          Full Screen Intent & Lock Screen Display
          Used to show incoming call screens even when the device is locked.
          
          System Alert Window
          Used only to display incoming call UI similar to other calling applications.
