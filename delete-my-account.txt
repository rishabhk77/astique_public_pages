In order to delete your account on Astique application. You can choose two options.
  Option 1 ) Use the application itself - Go to Profile Section > Tap 'Delete Account > Press "Confirm"
  Option 2 ) You can send us an email from your registered email address and we will delete the account for you

Please Note - Once an account is deleted we delete all the data including your details includinf email , mobile mobile number and country from our database.
              We will also delete your application data like call logs, membership details and subscription details.
              
You can contact us on - support@astique.co
